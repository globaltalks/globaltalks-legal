Community Guidelines

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

GlobalTalks is a platform for human connection across languages. These guidelines help keep our community safe, respectful, and welcoming for everyone.

1. Be Respectful

  • Treat all users with respect regardless of language, culture, or nationality
  • No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
  • No hate speech based on race, religion, gender, nationality, or disability
  • Disagreements should be handled respectfully

2. Prohibited Content

The following are strictly prohibited:
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Sexual or explicit content
  • Violence or threats
  • Terrorism or extremist content
  • Child exploitation or abuse
  • Self-harm promotion
  • Spam or scam messages
  • Misinformation or fake news
  • Illegal activities

3. Chat Rules

  • Only chat with people you know or have consented to connect with
  • Do not share personal information of others without consent
  • No unsolicited commercial messages (spam)
  • Voice messages must follow same content rules
  • Photos shared must be appropriate and legal

4. Emergency SOS Rules

Important: The Emergency SOS feature is for genuine emergencies only.
  • Do not misuse SOS for non-emergency situations
  • False emergency alerts may result in account suspension
  • Always call official emergency services (112) in life-threatening situations
  • GlobalTalks SOS is a supplementary tool, not a replacement for official services

5. AI and MOM Usage

  • Do not attempt to manipulate MOM AI with harmful prompts
  • Do not use AI features to generate harmful or illegal content
  • AI translations may not be 100% accurate — verify important translations
  • Voice cloning must only use your own voice or voice with explicit consent

6. Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the in-app report feature (long press on message)
  • Email us at globaltalks2026@gmail.com
  • We review all reports within 48 hours

7. Consequences of Violations

  • Warning: First violation — written warning
  • Temporary suspension: Repeated violations — 7 to 30 days
  • Permanent ban: Severe violations — immediate permanent ban
  • Legal action: Illegal content — reported to authorities

8. Appeals

If you believe your account was suspended in error, contact us at globaltalks2026@gmail.com with your appeal within 30 days.
