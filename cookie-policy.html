Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how GlobalTalks uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website globaltalksapp.com and web application.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for app to function — authentication, session management Session
Analytics Cookies Firebase Analytics — understand how users use the app 2 years
Performance Cookies Firebase Performance — monitor app speed and errors 1 year
Google Maps Cookies Required for Emergency SOS location features Session

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use these third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and analytics cookies
  • Google Maps — Location services cookies
  • ElevenLabs — Voice services

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings — Block or delete cookies in your browser
  • App settings — Disable analytics in GlobalTalks settings
  • Google Analytics Opt-out — Install Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect app functionality.

5. Mobile App

Our mobile app uses similar tracking technologies including:

  • Firebase SDK for analytics and crash reporting
  • Local storage for app preferences
  • Secure storage for authentication tokens

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
