Welcome to GlobalTalks. This Privacy Policy explains how GlobalTalks ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and website at globaltalksapp.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, username, and password

  • Voice and Audio Data: Voice recordings for speech-to-text translation and voice cloning features
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, device information, and analytics data
  • Chat Messages: Encrypted messages sent through our platform
  • Uploaded Content: Photos, documents, and images uploaded for translation

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide real-time translation services across 200+ languages
  • Enable AI companion (MOM) features
  • Provide Emergency SOS services and location-based assistance
  • Enable user-to-user encrypted chat messaging
  • Send push notifications and administrative information
  • Improve our AI translation accuracy
  • Respond to user inquiries and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

4. Device Permissions

GlobalTalks requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: For OCR text extraction and translation
  • Microphone: For voice translation and voice messages
  • Storage: For saving translations and documents
  • Location: For Emergency SOS GPS detection
  • SMS: For sending emergency location messages

5. Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • AES-256 encryption for all chat messages
  • HTTPS/SSL for all data transmission
  • Firebase security rules for database access
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Location data is not permanently stored — used only during emergency SOS
  • Voice data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Usage analytics are retained for 12 months
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is deleted within 30 days
